Let’s have another example with cubic and quadratic beziers.

Here the bottom of this bell is defined with straight lines.

<Example>

<svg width="200" height="200" viewBox="-100 -100 200 200" stroke="black" stroke-width="2">
  <path
      d="
        M -50 40
        L -50 50
        L 50 50
        L 50 40"
      fill="#FDEA96"
    />
</svg>

{/* prettier-ignore */}

</Example>

Then a quadratic Beziers starts the bell cloak.

<Example>

<svg width="200" height="200" viewBox="-100 -100 200 200" stroke="black" stroke-width="2">
  <path
    d="
      M -50 40
      L -50 50
      L 50 50
      L 50 40
      Q 40 40 40 10"
    fill="#FDEA96"
  />
  <circle 
    cx="40"
    cy="40"
    r="2" 
    stroke="none"
    fill="red"
  />
</svg>

{/* prettier-ignore */}
```html
<svg 
  width="200"
  height="200"
  viewBox="-100 -100 200 200"
  stroke="black"
  stroke-width="2"
>
  <path
    d="
      M -50 40
      L -50 50
      L 50 50
      L 50 40
      Q 40 40 40 10"
    fill="#FDEA96"
  />
</svg>
```

</Example>

Then the line is continued with a cubic Bezier to form the top of the bell.

<Example>

<svg width="200" height="200" viewBox="-100 -100 200 200" stroke="black" stroke-width="2">
  <path
    d="
      M -50 40
      L -50 50
      L 50 50
      L 50 40
      Q 40 40 40 10
      C 40 -60 -40 -60 -40 10"
    fill="#FDEA96"
  />
  <circle 
    cx="-40"
    cy="-60"
    r="2" 
    stroke="none"
    fill="red"
  />
  <circle 
    cx="40"
    cy="-60"
    r="2" 
    stroke="none"
    fill="red"
  />
</svg>

{/* prettier-ignore */}
```html
<svg
```

Then we reach the bottom part with another quadratic bezier that mirrors the previous one.

  />

We finish the bell, by adding two circles for the bell-clapper, and the hanger.
